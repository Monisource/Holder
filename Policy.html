Privacy and Security Policy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy and Security

At Clickstop, we take the privacy and security of our clients and their customers very seriously. Our policy is designed to ensure the utmost protection of your data and maintain the trust you place in us.

Key Points of Our Privacy and Security Policy

  • No Tracking or Monitoring: We do not track or monitor any customer portals outside of the official company setup and any purchased maintenance.
  • Data Control: Your store's data and customer information remain solely under your control.
  • Limited Access: Our team only accesses your site during the initial setup and for pre-arranged maintenance or support.
  • Secure Hosting: We use GitHub for hosting, ensuring high levels of security and reliability.
  • Encryption: All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard protocols.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits to ensure our systems remain robust and up-to-date.

Your Responsibilities

While we take extensive measures to protect your data, it's important that you also take steps to maintain security:

  • Keep your login credentials secure and do not share them.
  • Regularly update your passwords.
  • Be cautious when accessing your site from public networks.
  • Inform us immediately if you suspect any security breaches.

Data Handling

We only collect and store the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our services. This typically includes:

  • Your store name and contact information
  • Website content you provide
  • Technical data required for hosting and maintaining your site

We do not have access to or store any of your customers' personal or financial information.

Important: If you choose to integrate third-party services (e.g., payment processors), please review their privacy policies as well. While we can advise on best practices, we are not responsible for the data handling of external services.

Compliance

Our practices are designed to comply with relevant data protection regulations. However, as laws vary by region, we recommend consulting with a legal professional to ensure your overall e-commerce operations meet all applicable requirements.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify all clients of any significant changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy and security practices, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're here to help ensure you feel confident in the protection of your data.
