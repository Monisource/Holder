Pricing Structure

Our pricing is designed to provide maximum value while being accessible to small businesses:

  • Setup Fee: $30/hour for initial configuration and customization
  • Monthly Plans: Tiered options to suit different business needs and budgets
  • Custom Domain Support: Included in all plans for businesses with existing domains

Note: All plans include custom domain support. If you already own a domain, we'll help you integrate it seamlessly with your Clickstop-powered site.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Clickstop, we respect your business's privacy and autonomy. We do not track or monitor any customer portals outside of our official setup and purchased maintenance services. Your data and your customers' information remain under your control.
